## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and potential clients evaluating Naman Taneja for Data Engineering and Gen AI roles. They are looking for technical competence, enterprise experience, and professionalism.

### Brand Personality
Confident, Technical, Cinematic. The tone is professional but bold, highlighting enterprise scale and precision.

### Aesthetic Direction
Cinematic dark theme primarily, using warm-tinted off-blacks (terracotta hue). Accent colors are burnt rose/terracotta and muted steel-blue. Typography relies on Figtree for body, Barlow Semi Condensed for bold display headings, and Inconsolata for technical elements. 

### Design Principles
- **Cinematic Contrast**: Emphasize dramatic typography and stark contrast over busy elements.
- **Enterprise Polish**: Maintain a clean, highly structured, and readable layout suitable for enterprise data representation.
- **Warmth in Tech**: Use warm tints instead of stark, cold grays to make the portfolio feel human and approachable despite the technical focus.
